# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to providing a welcoming and inspiring community for all.
We expect all contributors and participants to adhere to this Code of Conduct.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to creating a positive environment include:

- 🤝 Being respectful and inclusive
- 💬 Using welcoming and inclusive language
- ✅ Accepting constructive criticism gracefully
- 🎯 Focusing on what is best for the community
- 🫂 Showing empathy towards others

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- ❌ Harassment or discrimination
- 💢 Insults, demeaning language, or personal attacks
- 🔒 Unwanted sexual attention or advances
- 🚨 Trolling or deliberate disruption
- 🔗 Sharing private information without consent
- 💼 Professional harassment in any form

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be
reported by contacting the project maintainers at:

📧 **conduct@koo-superdev.dev**  
🐛 **[GitHub Issues](https://github.com/koo/koo-superdev/issues)**

All reports will be reviewed and investigated promptly.

Maintainers are obligated to maintain confidentiality of the reporter.

## Consequences

Violations of this Code of Conduct may result in:

1. 📢 Warning
2. 🚫 Temporary suspension
3. ⛔ Permanent ban from community

The appropriate response will be determined on a case-by-case basis.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all community spaces including:

- GitHub issues and pull requests
- Project discussions and forums
- Social media interactions related to the project
- In-person events (if applicable)
- Any official communication channels
